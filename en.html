Who are we?

An association,
a shared passion

Born from a common love of song, our choir brings together voices from all walks of life in joy and good company.

The Crème Anglaise association brings together passionate singers in the Dinan-Évran area. Whether you are an experienced singer or a curious beginner wanting to discover choral singing, you are warmly welcome among us.

Our repertoire skilfully blends great English songs — pop, folk, soul — with gems of French chanson, creating joyful concerts that have the whole audience singing along.

Rehearsals take place in a relaxed and supportive atmosphere. No need to read music: all you need is the desire to sing and a good mood!

Ready to sing?

Join
the adventure

Crème Anglaise welcomes new members throughout the year. We are currently looking for singers: sopranos, altos, tenors, basses — all ages and nationalities welcome. No prior musical knowledge required — just enthusiasm!

📅

Weekly rehearsals

We rehearse every week in a relaxed atmosphere. Times are shared upon registration.

🎵

All voice parts

Sopranos, altos, tenors, basses — all registers are welcome. All ages and nationalities are valued in our ensemble.

💰

Annual membership

Affordable association membership, accessible to all. Contact us for the current season fee.

🤝

Come and try!

Feel free to come to a first rehearsal with no commitment. You'll leave with a smile.

Contact us

A question, a desire to join the choir, a request for a concert? Write to us — we'll be happy to reply.

The Crème Anglaise association is based in the Évran and Dinan area, in Côtes-d'Armor, Brittany. We are available for any enquiry and will be delighted to welcome you.
